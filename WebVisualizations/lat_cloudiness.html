For this plot, we are looking at the relationship between latitude and cloudiness. There is no clear correlation, however there are areas in the plot that suggest that particular percentages of cloudiness are more common, namely 0%, around 70% and around 90% cloudiness within the ranges of -60 degrees and around 70 degrees latitude. Other than the aforementioned observation, the data points are spread out evenly which suggest that there is no correlation between cloudiness and latitude.
